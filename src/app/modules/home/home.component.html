LYRICSET

LyricSet is a web-app designed for singers to help them organize their repertoire. With our help you can find any lyrics that you want and keep them well-organized inside your Library. LyricSet also gives you an opportunity to share your favourite tracks with other users by creating Bands, and allows you to rate other libraries.
